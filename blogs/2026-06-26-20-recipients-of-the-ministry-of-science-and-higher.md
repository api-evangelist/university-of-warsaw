The Minister of Science and Higher Education has announced this year’s list of recipients of the scholarship for outstanding young researchers. Among the recipients are 20 winners from the University of Warsaw. Out of 1,640 applications for scholarships for outstanding young researchers who have demonstrated significant achievements in their academic work, 230 recipients were selected.…
