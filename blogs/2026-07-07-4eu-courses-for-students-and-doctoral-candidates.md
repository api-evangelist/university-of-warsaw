Partner universities from the 4EU+ Alliance have reopened registration for their courses for students and doctoral candidates. There are classes available at the universities: Charles, Sorbonne, and Geneva. In the 2026/2027 academic year, the 4EU+ Alliance universities will once again launch online or hybrid courses for students and doctoral candidates from all eight member universities.…
